T.Rex: The Ultimate Predator

I implemented the depth-sensing technology required for animated dinosaurs on-screen to interact with humans, as well as many of the actions that they take, such as roaring, chasing humans, and interacting with other creatures.

Unseen Oceans

Unseen Oceans is an interactive exhibit in AMNH. Using technology, it examines deep-sea life and habitats in the inhospitable Mariana Trench thousands of feet beneath sea level.

I worked on the exhibit's arcade game that allows users to collect endangered coral samples in a submarine. I used Unity and C# to program aspects of the game including unit collision and language translations.

Sentiment Analyzer

Mental health is a rising issue in today's world, and many depressed people take to social media platforms to air out their frustrations and problems.

I created a bot in Java that scrapes tweets on a Twitter feed and analyzes their sentiment to check for suicidal thoughts or tendencies to identify users that may be in danger and provide assistance to them.

Yelp Dataset Challenge

Nearly all restaurants today have a Yelp page that allows users to leave reviews, comments and feedback about the businesses.

I participated in the Yelp Dataset Challenge and created a series of algorithms using Python that expanded upon Naïve Bayes' theorem and various other libraries to analyze the intent behind each review provide restaurants with useful feedback.

Instrument Exchange

Instrument Exchange is an online web platform where users can barter with one another for instruments, studio space, and other musical equipment. I worked extensively on the backend, using Express and Node to set up the authentication system, location services, and interaction between different user accounts.

Lookup to Lifecycle and State

Objects in Veeva's Vault platform implement lifecycles, which allow for useful operations such as tracking the state of a patient's prescription or drug's development.

I implemented the ability for objects to reference lifecycles and track/update their states (e.g. Active, Needs Restocking, Completed) asynchronously, allowing multiple objects to track the status of one another.

Banker's Algorithm

My implementation of Banker's Algorithm, an algorithm that prevents deadlocks in an operating system.

News in Chunks

News in Chunks is an iOS app created in Swift that brings a streamlined take on news; rather than browsing through clunky menus or UIs, the user sets their preferences then the app collects articles in chunks for the user to scroll through and read.

I implemented the backend logic of the app, including scraping from news sources, the data structures behind holding the articles, and the UITableViewControllers.
